Solution: CompactProductCard for carousel sections

New Component:
  ✓ CompactProductCard.tsx (174 lines)
  • Dimensions: 140x280px (26% smaller than ProductCard)
  • Square 140x140px image (better thumbnails)
  • Essential info only (restaurant, dish, price, time)

Architecture:
  Featured Section: CompactProductCard carousel

  Recommended Section: CompactProductCard carousel

Result:

  • Vertical scrolling: 1200px → 800px (-33%)
  • Better mobile experience
  • Industry-standard carousel UX
