🎨 DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Professional UI/UX Principles Applied:

1. Visual Hierarchy
   • Large images draw attention
   • Floating labels positioned for readability
   • Information density balanced

2. Motion & Engagement
   • Auto-rotation keeps content fresh

3. User Control
   • Pause on interaction respects user intent
   • Auto-resume provides hands-free experience
   • Indicators show carousel state

4. Information Architecture

🎓 DESIGN DECISIONS EXPLAINED
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Why Auto-Rotate Every 2 Seconds?
  • 2 seconds is optimal for food discovery
  • Long enough to read all text
  • Short enough to keep engagement
  • Industry standard for carousels

Why Pause on Interaction?
  • Respects user intent
  • Prevents jarring transitions
  • Allows deliberate browsing
  • Professional feel

Why 3-Second Resume Delay?
  • Gives user time to read current item
  • Resumes smoothly without jarring
  • Not too long (doesn't feel stuck)
  • Not too short (respects browsing)

Why Floating Labels?
  • Overlaid content saves space
  • Looks professional
  • Information accessible immediately
  • Doesn't obscure image

Why Separate Search Bar?
  • Cleaner visual hierarchy
  • Search has own focus area
  • Less cluttered header
  • Better usability
